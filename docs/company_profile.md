# LumiCart Company Profile

## 1. Company Overview

**Company name:** LumiCart

**Industry:** E-commerce

**Business model:** Business-to-consumer online marketplace

**Headquarters:** Berlin, Germany

**Founded:** 2021

LumiCart is a fictional European e-commerce platform created to simulate realistic Product Analytics and Data Analytics business problems.

The platform allows customers to browse products, add items to their shopping carts, complete purchases, and interact with the platform through both web and mobile applications.

## 2. Mission

To make online shopping simple, personalized, and reliable for customers across Europe.

## 3. Vision

To become a customer-centric e-commerce platform that uses data to continuously improve the shopping experience.

## 4. Product Categories

LumiCart offers products in the following categories:

* Electronics
* Fashion
* Beauty
* Home and Living
* Sports
* Pet Supplies

## 5. Target Customers

LumiCart mainly serves customers between 18 and 45 years old in European markets.

The initial focus markets are:

* Germany
* Austria
* Netherlands

## 6. Revenue Streams

LumiCart generates revenue through:

* Product sales
* Delivery fees
* Premium membership fees
* Advertising and sponsored product placements

## 7. Main Business Departments

* Executive Management
* Product
* Marketing
* Finance
* Operations
* Customer Success
* Engineering
* Data and Analytics

## 8. Core Business Challenges

The company wants to understand:

* Why users leave before completing a purchase
* Why some customer groups have low retention
* Which acquisition channels bring valuable customers
* Which customers are likely to churn
* Which products and categories drive revenue
* Whether product changes improve conversion

## 9. Analytics Objectives

The Product Analytics team is responsible for:

* Monitoring product KPIs
* Investigating conversion problems
* Measuring customer engagement
* Analyzing retention and cohorts
* Evaluating product experiments
* Segmenting customers
* Predicting customer churn
* Communicating insights to stakeholders

## 10. Disclaimer

LumiCart is a fictional company created for educational and portfolio purposes. Business assumptions will be adjusted when the final dataset is selected.
